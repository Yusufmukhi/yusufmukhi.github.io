What is a compactor?

A compactor is a device or machine used to compress or compact materials, typically to reduce their volume. In the context of waste management, a compactor is used to compress trash or waste into a smaller, more manageable size, which helps reduce the frequency of disposal and saves space. In other areas, such as construction or storage, compactors are used to compress soil, gravel, or other materials to create a more solid and stable surface.
